Our Mission

Build for the safety of the future.

Gatito Labs prioritizes privacy, sustainability, and user safety across every product decision.

Priority

Safety before scale.

We have one main goal that we prioritize with all of our services: the safety of the future. That means building products that are useful without normalizing tracking, abuse, or hidden costs.

Infrastructure

Cleaner systems by default.

Our Gatito infrastructure runs on renewable energy, has zero carbon emissions, and does not use a cloud provider. The goal is to keep the stack eco-friendly and morally aligned with the products it supports.

Standard

Useful should not mean invasive.

Free software can be transparent, careful, and pleasant to use. Our mission is to make that feel normal instead of exceptional.
