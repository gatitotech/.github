How Corporate Are We?

We try not to be.

Business can be on the same level as normal people. We are no different.

Gatito Labs is small, independent, and intentionally close to the products it builds. We do not want the company to feel distant from the people using its software.

In the end, we are normal people running this place. That shows up in the tone, the product decisions, and the belief that a company can be useful without becoming cold or extractive.

Small By Design

Less ceremony, more care.

A smaller company can move deliberately, talk plainly, and keep product decisions closer to user needs.

Plainspoken

No corporate fog.

We prefer honest language, readable policies, and products that explain themselves through how they work.
