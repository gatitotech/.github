Why We Work

We work for you.

Our free apps are intended to do more good than the secret, hidden bad every other "free" thing does.

We want that norm to change. Free products should not need to quietly profile people, make the interface worse, or treat users as raw material for someone else's growth plan.

Gatito Labs exists to build software that feels more direct: the product helps you, the privacy terms make sense, and the experience stays smooth because that is the whole point.

For users We design around the person using the app, not a hidden ad or data business.
For trust Simple limits, clear product behavior, and less background collection.
For better norms We want privacy-first free software to feel expected.
