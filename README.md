# Gatito Labs

## Who are we?
Gatito Labs is a small independent company with one goal in mind for the internet: free, privacy first, apps made to make user experiences better, safer, and smoother.

## Our Mission
We have one main goal that we prioritize with all of our services - the safety of the future. All of our Gatito infrastructure runs on renewable energy, has zero carbon emissions, and does not use any cloud provider to ensure that everything we do is eco-friendly and morally correct.

## Why we work
We work for you. All of our free apps are intended to do more good than the secret, hidden bad every other 'free' thing does. We want that norm to change.

## How corporate are we?
Well, we try to not be. We want to show that business can be on the same level as 'normal people.' We're no different! In the end, we are 'normal people' running this place.
